Rules & Regulations

  • Eligibility: Open to all students representing their respective institutions. A maximum of two participants per institution is allowed. Participants up to age 28 are eligible to participate.
  • Theme: The theme for the event is open. Participants are encouraged to showcase their creative liberty.
  • Date & Time: The event will be held on Thursday (10th July, 2025), from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Participants will be allotted a total of 4 hours (11AM to 3PM) to complete their artwork.
  • Medium & Materials: Canvas or A3-size art paper (11.7 x 16.5 inches) will be provided by the organizing committee. Basic art supplies will be provided at the venue. Other needed supplies are to be brought by the participants themselves. Only non-digital artwork is allowed (traditional mediums like watercolor, acrylic, pencil, etc.).
  • Submission: Artworks must have the participant's name and institution clearly written on the back. No identifying marks, names, or symbols should be visible on the front side of the artwork. All work must cease promptly at the end of the allotted time. Artwork submitted must not contain any depiction of alcohol consumption, alcoholic beverages, or smoking. Such submissions will be disqualified or removed.
  • Evaluation Criteria: Creativity & originality, Technique and artistic skill, Overall visual impact
  • Additional Rules: Use of reference images is allowed but no tracing or projection. Any form of obscene, offensive, or inappropriate content will result in disqualification. The judges' decisions are final and non-contestable.
  • Eligibility: Open to all college students (individual participation only). Maximum 2 participants per college.
  • Theme: "Stories in a Smile" - Submissions must clearly reflect the assigned theme.
  • Submission Guidelines: Each participant can submit only one original photo. Files must be submitted in JPEG/PNG format with high resolution (At least 8 x 10 inches at 300 DPI (i.e., ~2400 x 3000 pixels). Only basic image adjustments (brightness, contrast, saturation) are allowed. Manipulated or AI-generated images will be disqualified. A caption or description (maximum 50 words) is mandatory and must convey the participant's perspective or the essence of the image. Participants should submit their photographs by 7th July, 2025 (till 4pm). Photos submitted must not contain any depiction of alcohol consumption, alcoholic beverages, or smoking. Such submissions will be disqualified or removed.
  • Technical Requirements: File name should be: Name_College_Title.jpg. Entries must be original, taken by the participant, and free from copyright or third-party ownership. No watermarks, logos, or identifiable marks should be present on the image.
  • Evaluation Criteria: Relevance to theme, Creativity & storytelling, Composition & technique, Visual/ overall impact
  • Other Rules: Plagiarism or submission of copyrighted work will result in immediate disqualification.
